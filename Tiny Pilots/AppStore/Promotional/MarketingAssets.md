# Marketing Assets and Promotional Materials

## App Preview Video Specifications

### Technical Requirements
- **Duration**: 15-30 seconds
- **Format**: MP4 or MOV
- **Resolution**: Match screenshot dimensions for each device
- **Frame Rate**: 30 fps minimum
- **Audio**: Optional but recommended

### Video Content Strategy
1. **Opening (0-3s)**: App icon animation and title
2. **Gameplay Demo (3-20s)**: 
   - Show paper airplane launching and flying
   - Demonstrate tilt controls
   - Showcase beautiful environments
   - Include particle effects and visual polish
3. **Feature Highlights (20-25s)**:
   - Quick montage of different game modes
   - Show customization options
   - Display Game Center integration
4. **Closing (25-30s)**: Call to action and app name

### Video Guidelines
- Capture actual gameplay footage
- Use smooth, engaging camera movements
- Show the most visually appealing environments
- Demonstrate intuitive controls
- Keep pacing energetic but not overwhelming

## Marketing Copy

### Elevator Pitch (30 seconds)
"Tiny Pilots brings the nostalgic joy of paper airplanes to your iPhone with stunning realism. Our advanced physics engine captures every fold and crease as you soar through five beautiful environments. With Game Center integration, daily challenges, and full accessibility support, it's the premium paper airplane experience you've been waiting for."

### Key Selling Points
- **Realistic Physics**: Advanced simulation of paper airplane flight
- **Beautiful Environments**: Five distinct, stunning worlds to explore
- **Multiple Game Modes**: Free play, challenges, daily runs, weekly specials
- **Social Features**: Game Center leaderboards and achievements
- **Accessibility**: Full VoiceOver and assistive technology support
- **Premium Experience**: No ads, no in-app purchases, complete game

### Target Audience Messaging

#### Casual Gamers
"Relax and unwind with the simple joy of paper airplane flight. Beautiful graphics and intuitive controls make Tiny Pilots perfect for quick gaming sessions."

#### Flight Sim Enthusiasts  
"Experience authentic paper airplane physics with our advanced simulation engine. Every fold, crease, and wind current affects your flight path."

#### Families
"Share the wonder of flight with players of all ages. Tiny Pilots combines education and entertainment in a safe, ad-free environment."

#### Accessibility Community
"Fully accessible gaming experience with comprehensive VoiceOver support, dynamic type scaling, and assistive technology compatibility."
